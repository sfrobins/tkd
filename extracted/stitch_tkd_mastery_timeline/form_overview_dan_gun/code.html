THE LEGEND OF DAN-GUN

History tells of a heavenly prince, Hwan-ung, who descended to Earth. He transformed a bear into a woman who gave birth to Dan-Gun Wanggeom. Dan-Gun founded Gojoseon, the first Korean kingdom, establishing a legacy of discipline and wisdom that survives in every movement of this Tul.

"To learn Dan-Gun is to understand the foundation of the spirit."
