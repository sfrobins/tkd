Ahn Chang-Ho

Pattern: Do-San

The pseudonym of the patriot Ahn Chang-Ho (1876-1938). The 24 movements represent his entire life which he devoted to furthering the education of Korea and its independence movement.

Pattern: Won-Hyo

Named after the noted monk who introduced Buddhism to the Silla Dynasty in the year 686 A.D. The pattern reflects the spiritual discipline of the scholar-monks.
