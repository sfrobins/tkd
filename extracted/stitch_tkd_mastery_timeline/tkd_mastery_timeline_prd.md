# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
**Product Name:** TKD Mastery Timeline – Journey with Grand Master Scott Craig

## Product Overview
TKD Mastery Timeline is a modern, interactive web platform dedicated to teaching the International Tae Kwon Do Federation (ITF) style of Tae Kwon Do. It centers on an immersive **visual timeline** that represents a student’s progressive journey from beginner (white belt) to advanced mastery (up to 3rd Dan black belt).

The site lets users **experience** the 12 core ITF forms listed by Grand Master Scott Craig:
- Chon-Ji (9th Gup)
- Dan-Gun (8th Gup)
- Do-San (7th Gup)
- Won-Hyo (6th Gup)
- Yul-Gok (5th Gup)
- Joong-Gun (4th Gup)
- Toi-Gye (3rd Gup)
- Hwa-Rang (2nd Gup)
- Choong-Moo (1st Gup)
- Kwang-Gae (1st Degree Black Belt)
- Poe-Eun (2nd Degree Black Belt)
- Ge-Baek (3rd Degree Black Belt)

Each milestone on the timeline combines:
- High-quality instruction in the form itself
- Real-time coaching in essential Korean vocabulary and commands
- Deep integration of Korean cultural and historical context

## Vision
To create the most engaging, respectful, and effective online platform for learning ITF Tae Kwon Do—where students not only master the physical forms but also internalize the language, etiquette, philosophy, and cultural roots of the art under Grand Master Scott Craig’s guidance.

## Key Features
1. **Interactive Mastery Timeline**: Visually rich, scrollable timeline with 12 milestone nodes.
2. **Form Learning Modules**: Overview, Demonstration Videos, Step-by-Step Breakdown, Practice Mode, Self-Assessment, and Cultural Tie-In.
3. **Korean Vocabulary Coaching**: Integrated & Progressive glossary and tools.
4. **Korean Culture Showcase**: Dedicated heritage hub.
5. **User Account & Progress System**: Personalized dashboard and progress tracking.
6. **Supporting Pages**: About GM Scott Craig, Community, Resources.

## Design & Branding Guidelines
- **Color palette**: Black, white, red (traditional dobok/Taekwon-Do), with gold accents and subtle Korean patterns.
- **Tone**: Respectful, encouraging, disciplined yet approachable.
- **Visuals**: Traditional Korean scroll or dojang timeline motifs (Taeguk symbols, hanji paper texture).
